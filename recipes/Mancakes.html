Mancakes

Home

Description:

Mancakes are hearty, protein-packed pancakes typically made with ingredients like oats, eggs, and bananas for a nutritious, filling meal.
They're a healthy twist on traditional pancakes, often enjoyed as a post-workout breakfast or muscle-building snack.

Ingredients:

  • Rolled Oats
  • Ripe banana
  • Egg
  • Baking Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Protein Powder
  • Almond Milk or Milk
  • Pinch of Salt

Steps to cook:

  1. Blend Ingridients

    2. Add oats, banana, eggs, baking powder, vanilla, cinnamon, protein powder (if using), milk, and salt into a blender. Blend until smooth.

  2. Heat the Pan

    3. Preheat a non-stick skillet or pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with oil or cooking spray.

  3. Cook the pancakes

    4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan. Cook for 2–3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface

  4. Flip and finish

    5. Flip and cook the other side for 1–2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through

  5. Serve

    6. Stack and serve warm with toppings like fresh fruit, nut butter, or Greek yogurt.

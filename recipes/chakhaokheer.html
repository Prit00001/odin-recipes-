Chak Hao Kheer(Black rice kheer)

Description:

Chak Hao Kheer is a traditional Manipuri dessert made from aromatic black rice, simmered in milk and sweetened with sugar or jaggery. It’s known for its rich purple color, nutty flavor, and high nutritional value.

Ingredients:

  • Chak Hao (black rice) – 1/2 cup
  • Milk – 3–4 cups
  • Sugar or jaggery – 1/2 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) – 2–3 tbsp
  • Raisins – 1 tbsp
  • Ghee (clarified butter) – 1 tbsp
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Soak the rice

    2. Rinse and soak 1/2 cup black rice (Chak Hao) for 3–4 hours or overnight.

  2. Cook the Rice

    3. Boil soaked rice in 1 cup water until soft and partially cooked

  3. Add Milk

    4. Pour in 3–4 cups of milk and simmer on low heat, stirring often until the rice is fully cooked and the mixture thickens.

  4. Add sweetener and flavor

    5. Stir in sugar or jaggery, cardamom powder, and simmer for a few more minutes until well bl

  5. Garnish and serve

    6. In ghee, fry nuts and raisins, then mix them into the kheer. Serve warm or chilled.
